隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 5 月 3 日

本隱私權政策適用於 Yu-Hsiang Chang 發布的 App 與服務，包括向量財管 VectraFin、我的台灣觀光地圖、英語救星、台北菜價、師記、Melody Journal 與 Notelyra；若特定 App 提供獨立隱私權政策，則以該政策為準。

Sticker Vault 與注音故事各有專屬隱私權政策，請參閱其 App 頁面連結。

不蒐集個人資料的 App

向量財管 VectraFin、英語救星、師記、Melody Journal、Notelyra 與我的台灣觀光地圖不會透過開發者營運的伺服器向使用者蒐集個人資料。

向量財管 VectraFin

向量財管 VectraFin 會在使用者裝置上處理使用者自行輸入的帳戶名稱、交易金額、Hashtags、備註、日期、週期帳設定、統計查詢條件、資產或負債紀錄，以及由這些資料產生的圖表、趨勢與明細。這些資料用於記帳、資產清點、負債追蹤、週期帳預測、Hashtag 階層分析與視覺化功能。

開發者不會透過自己的伺服器收集、上傳或檢視使用者在 VectraFin 中建立的財務資料。資料預設保存在使用者裝置上；若使用者選擇本機備份、iCloud 快照或 iCloud 同步，相關資料會由使用者裝置或 Apple iCloud 服務依使用者的系統設定與 Apple 的隱私權實務處理。

VectraFin 不要求註冊帳號，也不會建立開發者營運的外部伺服器帳號。若使用者刪除 App、刪除帳戶、關閉同步或管理備份，資料保留與刪除結果會依 App 操作、裝置設定與 iCloud 設定而定。

我的台灣觀光地圖

我的台灣觀光地圖會在使用者授權後讀取相簿中照片的 GPS 座標與拍攝時間，用於判斷景點是否已造訪、分組旅程訪次、建立景點相簿、顯示成就統計與整理計劃清單。照片內容、座標比對結果、旅行回憶與日誌內容以裝置端處理為核心，開發者不會透過自己的伺服器收集或上傳使用者的照片、相簿內容或 GPS 足跡。

使用者可使用 iOS 內建的 Writing Tools 協助撰寫旅遊日誌，也可透過系統 Share Sheet 分享相簿與日誌。這些功能由 Apple 或使用者選擇的分享目的地處理，請同時參考 Apple 與相關第三方服務的隱私實務。

App 使用政府或公開來源的觀光資料來呈現景點資訊與分類。這些公開資料不包含使用者個人資料，且資料內容可能由來源單位更新。

Notelyra

Notelyra 會在裝置上處理課程錄音、產生的筆記、樂器選擇、標題、時間標籤與關鍵字編輯，用於錄音、轉錄、練習回顧與音樂探索功能。開發者不會向使用者蒐集這些內容。

當使用者開啟 Apple Music 推薦或串流播放至 HomePod 時，相關功能由 Apple 提供，並依 Apple 的隱私權實務、帳號設定與媒體服務條款處理。

台北菜價與廣告

台北菜價使用 Google 廣告服務。App 本身並非為直接蒐集使用者個人資料而設計，但 Google 可能依其隱私權政策處理裝置或廣告相關資訊以投放廣告。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to apps and services published by Yu-Hsiang Chang, including VectraFin, MyTaiwanTourMap, 英語救星, 台北菜價, 師記, Melody Journal, and Notelyra, unless a specific app provides a separate privacy notice.

Sticker Vault and 注音故事 have their own privacy policies linked from their app pages.

Apps Without Personal Data Collection

VectraFin, 英語救星, 師記, Melody Journal, Notelyra, and MyTaiwanTourMap do not collect personal data from users through developer-operated servers.

VectraFin

VectraFin processes account names, transaction amounts, hashtags, notes, dates, recurring-entry settings, statistics query conditions, asset or liability records, and the charts, trends, and details generated from that information on the user's device. This information is used for bookkeeping, asset review, liability tracking, recurring-entry forecasting, hierarchical hashtag analysis, and visualization features.

Yu-Hsiang Chang does not collect, upload, or view financial records created by users in VectraFin through developer-operated servers. Data is stored on the user's device by default. If the user chooses local backups, iCloud snapshots, or iCloud sync, the relevant data is handled by the user's device or Apple's iCloud services according to the user's system settings and Apple's privacy practices.

VectraFin does not require account registration and does not create developer-operated server accounts. If a user deletes the app, deletes an account, turns off sync, or manages backups, data retention and deletion results depend on the app action, device settings, and iCloud settings.

MyTaiwanTourMap

After the user grants permission, MyTaiwanTourMap reads GPS coordinates and capture dates from photos in the user's photo library to determine visited attractions, group trip visits, build place albums, show achievement statistics, and manage planning lists. Photo content, coordinate matching results, travel memories, and journal content are designed around on-device processing. Yu-Hsiang Chang does not collect or upload users' photos, album content, or GPS travel history through developer-operated servers.

Users may use Apple's built-in Writing Tools to help draft travel journals and may share albums or journal content through the system Share Sheet. These features are handled by Apple or by the sharing destination selected by the user, so Apple's and relevant third-party privacy practices may also apply.

The app uses government or public tourism data to display attraction information and categories. This public data does not contain user personal data and may be updated by the source providers.

Notelyra

Notelyra processes lesson recordings, generated notes, instrument selections, titles, time tags, and keyword edits on device. These items are used to provide recording, transcription, practice review, and music discovery features. Yu-Hsiang Chang does not collect this content from users.

When users open Apple Music recommendations or stream playback to HomePod, those features are provided by Apple and handled under Apple privacy practices, account settings, and media service terms.

台北菜價 and Advertising

台北菜價 uses Google advertising services. While the app itself is not intended to collect personal data directly from users, Google may process device-related or advertising-related information under its own systems and privacy policy in order to deliver ads.

How Information Is Used

  • To respond to support inquiries you send by email
  • To keep the apps functioning properly
  • To provide VectraFin on-device bookkeeping, account management, hierarchical hashtag statistics, recurring-entry forecasts, chart visualization, local backups, iCloud snapshots, and iCloud sync features
  • To provide MyTaiwanTourMap on-device photo location matching, visit grouping, achievement statistics, place albums, travel journals, and sharing features
  • To provide Notelyra on-device lesson recording, note generation, practice review, and music discovery features
  • To display ads in 台北菜價 through Google advertising services
  • To comply with legal obligations if required

Data Retention and Rights

If you contact support, your email and related correspondence may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain support records. You may contact us regarding privacy-related questions or requests where applicable under local law.

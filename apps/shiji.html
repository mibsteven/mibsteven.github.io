師記

師記是一款為教師設計的隱私安全 AI 課堂管理助手。它整合 Apple Intelligence，協助老師把課堂紀錄轉成聯絡簿草稿、學生表現報告與可回顧的教學摘要，讓日常行政工作更輕、更有秩序。

核心亮點

  • 使用 Apple Intelligence 自動引用課堂紀錄，產生學生報告與聯絡簿草稿
  • 無需申請 API Key，也不需要登入或綁定 AI 帳號
  • 資料主要儲存在裝置本機，AI 運算不經過開發者雲端伺服器
  • 支援課堂狀況隨手記錄，並可整理成回顧摘要
  • 提供出缺勤管理、課程紀錄區塊與快速搜尋，方便查找課程與學生資料
  • 生成的草稿可快速銜接通訊工具，節省聯絡簿訊息撰寫時間

適合誰使用

師記適合需要管理多堂課、追蹤學生狀況、整理出缺勤與撰寫家長溝通訊息的老師。它把繁瑣紀錄集中在清楚的介面中，讓老師把更多時間留給教學本身。

平台與訂閱

App Store 顯示師記支援 iPhone、iPad、配備 Apple M1 或以上晶片的 Mac，以及 Apple Vision。師記提供 App 內購買的 Pro 訂閱方案，包含月度與年度專業版。

隱私

App Store 隱私資訊顯示，開發者不會從此 App 收集任何資料。師記的設計重點是本機管理與 On-Device AI，以降低師生資料外流風險。

師記

師記 is a privacy-minded AI classroom management assistant for teachers. It integrates Apple Intelligence to help turn classroom notes into contact-book drafts, student performance reports, and reviewable teaching summaries, making daily administrative work lighter and more organized.

Core Highlights

  • Uses Apple Intelligence to reference classroom records and generate student reports or contact-book drafts
  • Requires no API key, AI account login, or external AI account setup
  • Keeps data primarily on the local device, with AI processing designed to avoid the developer's cloud servers
  • Supports quick classroom notes and AI-assisted review summaries
  • Includes attendance management, structured course record sections, and fast search for lessons or student information
  • Lets teachers quickly move generated drafts into communication tools to save time on parent updates

Who It Is For

師記 is designed for teachers who need to manage multiple classes, track student status, organize attendance, and write parent communication messages. It keeps classroom records in a clear interface so teachers can spend more attention on teaching.

Platforms and Subscription

The App Store lists support for iPhone, iPad, Mac with Apple M1 or later, and Apple Vision. 師記 offers in-app Pro subscriptions with monthly and yearly plans.

Privacy

The App Store privacy listing states that the developer does not collect data from this app. 師記 focuses on local management and on-device AI to reduce privacy risk for teacher and student information.

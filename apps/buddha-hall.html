佛堂

《佛堂》是一款以 Apple Vision Pro 為核心設計的沉浸式修行空間 App。我們希望把日常讀經、迴向、祈願與靜心，放進一個安定、莊嚴而容易進入的數位佛堂中。

使用者戴上 Apple Vision Pro 後，可以進入完整的 3D 佛堂環境，在主壇前讀誦經文、聆聽音樂、完成迴向，或以不同視角巡禮整個空間。

觀音主題佛堂

第一版以觀世音菩薩為主題，內建多部常用經文，包括《般若波羅蜜多心經》、《觀世音菩薩普門品》、《金剛般若波羅蜜經》、大悲咒、觀音聖號與迴向文。使用者也可以自行加入經文，建立更貼近個人修行習慣的內容。

讀經、迴向與祈願

在讀誦完成後，App 會引導使用者進入迴向流程。迴向對象與內容皆保存在本機，不會上傳到伺服器。使用者也可以透過「祈願日記」記錄自己的願望、心念與修行方向。

音樂與共修

佛堂支援 Apple Music 搜尋與播放，也可以播放本機音訊檔，讓使用者在佛堂中搭配佛曲、經文、靜心音樂或自己準備的音訊。若使用 SharePlay，使用者還可以與同樣安裝 App 的朋友一起進入共修狀態，共同讀經、巡禮與完成儀式。

本機修行紀錄

App 會在本機記錄使用統計，例如來到佛堂的次數、停留時間、讀誦過的經文、每部經讀誦次數，以及完成迴向的紀錄。這些資料用來幫助使用者回顧自己的修行歷程，不會用於追蹤或廣告。

iPhone 與 iPad

iPhone 與 iPad 版本提供簡化的 2D 體驗，使用者可以管理經文、查看統計、記錄祈願，並作為進入 Vision Pro 沉浸式佛堂前的日常入口。

設計理念

佛堂不是要取代真實寺院或宗教儀式，而是希望在現代生活裡，提供一個可以隨時安住片刻的空間。無論是讀一部經、念一句佛號、寫下一個願望，或只是靜靜坐一會兒，都能在這裡完成。

Buddha Hall

Buddha Hall is an immersive Buddhist practice space designed around Apple Vision Pro. It brings daily sutra reading, dedication, wishes, and quiet reflection into a digital hall that feels calm, dignified, and easy to enter.

On Apple Vision Pro, users can enter a complete 3D Buddhist hall, read sutras at the main altar, listen to music, complete dedication, or explore the space from different viewpoints.

A Guanyin-Themed Practice Space

The first version is themed around Guanyin Bodhisattva and includes commonly used texts such as the Heart Sutra, Universal Gate Chapter, Diamond Sutra, Great Compassion Mantra, Guanyin recitation, and dedication text. Users can also add their own sutras to better match personal practice habits.

Sutra Reading, Dedication, and Wishes

After reading, the app guides users into a dedication flow. Dedication recipients and content are stored locally and are not uploaded to a server. Users can also record wishes, intentions, and practice direction in a wish journal.

Music and Shared Practice

Buddha Hall supports Apple Music search and playback, and can also play local audio files. Users can pair the hall with Buddhist music, sutra audio, meditation music, or prepared personal audio. With SharePlay, users who have the app installed can enter a shared practice state to read, explore, and complete rituals together.

Local Practice Records

The app records usage statistics locally, such as visit count, time spent in the hall, sutras read, reading count for each sutra, and completed dedication records. This information helps users review their own practice journey and is not used for tracking or advertising.

iPhone and iPad

The iPhone and iPad versions provide a simplified 2D experience for managing sutras, reviewing statistics, recording wishes, and serving as a daily entry point before entering the immersive Vision Pro hall.

Design Philosophy

Buddha Hall is not intended to replace real temples or religious ceremonies. It offers a space in modern life where users can settle for a moment, whether by reading one sutra, reciting one sacred name, writing down one wish, or simply sitting quietly.

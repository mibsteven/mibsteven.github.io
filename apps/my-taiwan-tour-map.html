我的台灣觀光地圖

我的台灣觀光地圖是一款結合自動足跡紀錄與智慧旅遊管家的個人化導覽 App。它以 Apple Maps 與台灣觀光開放資料為基礎，協助使用者把相簿裡帶有 GPS 座標的照片整理成一張專屬的台灣旅遊護照。

全台景點地圖

App 會將台灣觀光景點標示在地圖上，並以已造訪與未造訪狀態區分。使用者可以查看景點總數、已到訪數，以及各類別的走透透比例，掌握自己的足跡覆蓋率。

零操作足跡紀錄

在使用者授權相簿存取後，App 會比對照片 GPS 座標與景點位置。當照片座標落在合理範圍內，該景點會自動標記為已造訪。系統也會依照片時間分組，協助辨識同一次旅程、二訪與三訪等不同回憶。

旅遊回憶與相簿

每個景點都可以形成專屬動態相簿，集中瀏覽使用者在該地拍攝的照片。使用者可編輯旅遊回憶，也可使用裝置端 Writing Tools 協助完成日誌，並透過 iOS Share Sheet 分享相簿與文字到社群平台或其他 App。

分類探索與計劃清單

使用者可以依登山步道、古蹟、森林遊樂區等子類別探索，也能把想去的景點加入計劃中，讓下一趟台灣旅行更容易整理。

隱私優先

照片、座標比對、旅行回憶與日誌內容以裝置端處理為核心。開發者不會透過自己的伺服器收集使用者的照片、相簿內容或 GPS 足跡。

MyTaiwanTourMap

MyTaiwanTourMap is a personalized travel guide that combines automatic visit tracking with a smart Taiwan travel companion. Built around Apple Maps and Taiwan tourism open data, it helps turn GPS-tagged photos into a private digital travel passport.

Taiwan Attraction Map

The app places Taiwan attractions on a map and separates visited places from unvisited ones. Users can review total attraction counts, visited counts, and completion percentages for categories such as trails, heritage sites, and scenic areas.

Zero-Input Visit Tracking

After the user grants photo library permission, the app compares photo GPS coordinates with attraction locations. When a photo falls within a reasonable area around an attraction, that place can be marked as visited. Photo timestamps help group one trip separately from later return visits.

Travel Memories and Albums

Each attraction can become its own dynamic album of photos taken at that place. Users can edit travel memories, use on-device Writing Tools to help draft journal text, and share albums or journal content through the native iOS Share Sheet.

Category Discovery and Planning

Users can explore focused categories such as hiking trails, historic sites, and forest recreation areas, then save interesting places to a planning list for future trips.

Privacy First

Photo matching, location history, travel memories, and journal content are designed around on-device processing. Yu-Hsiang Chang does not collect users' photos, album content, or GPS travel history through developer-operated servers.

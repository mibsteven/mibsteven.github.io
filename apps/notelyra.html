Notelyra

Notelyra is a music lesson note-taking app designed for students who want to record lessons, review teacher guidance, and discover related music for listening practice.

Record

During a music lesson, students can choose the instrument being studied and start recording. Notelyra uses on-device AI to turn the recording into time-based notes, and the selected instrument helps the app use more relevant musical terms for better recognition.

Practice

At home, students can reopen a lesson recording and jump to teacher instructions by title or time tag. This makes it easier to replay specific moments and review technique, phrasing, or practice advice.

Listen

Notelyra extracts keywords from lesson recordings and uses them to recommend related music on Apple Music. Students can adjust keywords for more accurate searches, and playback can be streamed to HomePod where available.

Privacy

Notelyra processes lesson recordings and AI-generated notes on device. The app does not collect personal data from users. Apple Music and HomePod features are provided by Apple and are subject to Apple's own privacy practices.

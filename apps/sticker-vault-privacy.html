Sticker Vault Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sticker Vault app. Sticker Vault is designed to help you create, organize, and send personal stickers from your own photos while keeping processing on your device whenever possible.

Information We Access

Sticker Vault may access photos or images that you choose to import, along with sticker metadata such as tags or organization choices you create inside the app. The app only works with content you intentionally provide.

How Your Data Is Processed

Sticker creation features, including background removal, image optimization, and sticker preparation, are processed locally on your device. Your imported images and generated stickers are not uploaded to the developer's servers for processing.

Cloud Storage and Tracking

Sticker Vault does not store your images in the developer's cloud services. The app is designed not to track your activity for advertising or analytics purposes.

How Information Is Used

  • To import, process, organize, and display your stickers inside the app and iMessage extension
  • To save sticker-related data on your device so your collection remains available to you
  • To respond to support messages if you contact the developer by email
  • To comply with legal obligations if required

Data Storage

Your stickers, selected images, and related metadata are intended to remain on your device unless you choose to back up your device through Apple's services or other tools you control. The developer does not maintain a separate cloud copy of this content.

Third-Party Services

Sticker Vault is not designed to share your imported images with third-party analytics or advertising platforms. If the app relies on Apple system frameworks to access photos or present stickers in iMessage, those interactions are governed by Apple's platform behavior and your device settings.

Your Choices

You can control photo access through your device settings and remove app content by deleting stickers, removing imported items, or uninstalling the app. If you contact support, you may also request deletion of email correspondence where applicable.

Children's Privacy

Sticker Vault is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children through developer-operated servers. Because the app primarily processes content locally on the device, users should avoid importing sensitive personal information into stickers.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated if Sticker Vault's data practices materially change. The latest version will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Sticker Vault

Sticker Vault 可把照片快速整理成個人貼圖收藏，協助 iPhone 使用者建立、分類並直接在 iMessage 裡傳送自訂貼圖。

主要功能

  • 使用裝置端智慧功能快速去背，單次點擊即可分離照片主體
  • 以標籤管理貼圖，依家庭、工作、迷因或內部笑話分類
  • 支援 iMessage，能直接從訊息 App 抽屜尋找並傳送貼圖
  • 自動調整貼圖大小與壓縮品質，兼顧清晰度與傳送效率
  • 以隱私為優先，盡可能在本機處理，不把圖片上傳到開發者雲端

使用方式

  1. 從照片圖庫匯入圖片
  2. 透過自動去背與最佳化建立貼圖
  3. 使用自訂標籤整理貼圖
  4. 在 iMessage 中快速傳送

Sticker Vault

Turn your photos into your personal sticker collection instantly. Sticker Vault helps iPhone users create, organize, and send custom stickers directly inside iMessage without digging through a cluttered photo library.

Key Features

  • Instant background removal using on-device intelligence to isolate subjects from your photos in a single tap
  • Smart tagging and organization so your stickers stay sorted by categories like Family, Work, Memes, or Inside Jokes
  • Native iMessage integration that lets you find and send stickers directly from the iMessage app drawer
  • Quality-focused processing that automatically resizes and compresses stickers for iMessage while preserving visual clarity
  • Privacy-first design with local processing and no cloud storage of your images

How It Works

  1. Import photos from your library.
  2. Process each image with automatic background removal and sticker optimization.
  3. Organize your stickers with custom tags for faster access.
  4. Open iMessage and send the right sticker with a single tap.
